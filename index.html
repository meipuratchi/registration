About the Initiative

We guide students from 10th, 12th, and even those who have failed in their board exams by showing them proper government opportunities and career paths in Arts & Science, Engineering, Medicine, Music, Direction, etc.

Our Mission: To provide free, accessible career guidance to every government school student in Tamil Nadu, helping them discover their potential and find the right path for their future.

Opportunities & Exams

We provide comprehensive information about various career paths and examination opportunities:

Science Stream

  • NEET for Medical
  • JEE for Engineering
  • Research Careers

Arts Stream

  • Performing Arts
  • Literature & Languages
  • Social Sciences

Creative Fields

  • Music & Composition
  • Film Direction
  • Fine Arts

Eligibility

Students from Tamil Nadu

Priority to Government & Government Aided School Students

Required Documents: ID Card, Aadhar Card, 10th/12th Mark Sheet

85% of our beneficiaries are from government schools

How it Works

  1. Student registers on our website through a simple form
  2. Our dedicated team verifies the profile within 48 hours
  3. Confirmation message will be sent via SMS/Email
  4. One-on-one guidance session scheduled with our career counselor
  5. After course selection, student updates their status on our portal
  6. Follow-up support for 6 months after initial guidance

Trust & Privacy

This is a trust-based initiative run by volunteers who believe in giving back to society. Your data security is our priority:

We collect minimal information required for verification and guidance. All data is stored securely and never shared with third parties without consent.

Your information is used solely for career counseling purposes. We maintain anonymized statistics to improve our services but never disclose individual details.
