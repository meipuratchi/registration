Eligibility Criteria

Who Can Apply?

  • Minimum educational qualification: 10+2 (Higher Secondary) with Science stream
  • Must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as main subjects
  • Minimum aggregate marks required: 50% (45% for reserved categories)
  • Age limit: Minimum 17 years, Maximum 25 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
  • Medical fitness certificate required

Note: Some institutions may have additional requirements or entrance exams.

Cutoff Marks

Recent Year Cutoffs

The cutoff marks for DMLT programs vary by institution and category. Here are approximate ranges:

  • General Category: 60-75% in 10+2
  • OBC Category: 55-65% in 10+2
  • SC/ST Category: 45-55% in 10+2
  • Management Quota: Varies by college (usually lower but higher fees)

For entrance-based admissions, cutoff percentiles typically range between 85-95 percentile.
